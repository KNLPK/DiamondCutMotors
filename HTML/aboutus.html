WHO WE ARE

We Have 26 Years Of Experience In This Field

DiamondCut Motors was established in 1998 with a vision to redefine the luxury car resale market in Indonesia. From a humble beginning with a small showroom, the company has grown exponentially over the years. Today, DiamondCut Motors is synonymous with luxury, quality, and trust. Our extensive portfolio includes some of the most prestigious brands in the automotive industry such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many more. Our commitment to excellence has earned us a loyal customer base that includes numerous business owners and successful individuals.
